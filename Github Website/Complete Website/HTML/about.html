About Our School

Our Story, Values & Community

Our Purpose

Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic education that inspires academic excellence, nurtures creativity, and develops responsible citizens. We are committed to giving every learner the knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed while promoting respect, discipline, and Ubuntu in our school community.

Our Vision

To be a leading South African school recognized for empowering learners to become critical thinkers, ethical leaders, and active contributors to our diverse society. We envision graduates who are proud of their heritage while making positive contributions locally and globally.

Our Journey

Decades of educational excellence in our community

Rooted in Community

Established in 1976, Bajabulile Primary School began as a community initiative to provide quality education during challenging times. What started with just four classrooms and dedicated teachers has grown into one of the area's most respected educational institutions.

Over the years, we have adapted our curriculum to meet South Africa's changing educational landscape while maintaining strong community values. Our commitment to both academic excellence and character development has earned us recognition in district and provincial achievements.

Today, we proudly serve over 800 learners from Grade R to Grade 12, preparing them for further studies, vocational training, and meaningful careers. Our alumni include educators, healthcare workers, entrepreneurs, and professionals who contribute to building our nation.
