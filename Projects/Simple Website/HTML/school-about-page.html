About Thaba Bosigo Combined School

Our Story, Our Mission, Our People

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers learners to become confident, creative, and responsible citizens. We cultivate critical thinking, foster innovation, and instill values of Ubuntu that prepare our learners for success in South Africa and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of educational excellence in our community, recognized for nurturing well-rounded individuals who make meaningful contributions to their communities. We aspire to create an environment where every learner discovers their potential and pursues their passion with confidence and purpose.

Our Story

Founded in 1976, Thaba Bosigo Combined School began as a small community initiative with a big dream: to provide quality education during challenging times. Over the past 25 years, we have grown into a respected educational institution, serving over 800 learners from diverse backgrounds in our community.

Our journey has been marked by community support and unwavering commitment to excellence. From our humble beginnings in a few classrooms, we have expanded to a modern school featuring well-equipped classrooms, science laboratories, sports facilities, and creative arts spaces.

Today, Thaba Bosigo Combined School stands as a testament to what dedicated teachers, supportive families, and determined learners can achieve together. Our graduates have gone on to excel in universities and colleges across South Africa and make significant contributions in fields ranging from teaching and healthcare to business and public service.
